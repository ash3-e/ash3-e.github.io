Problem coverage
This set focuses on counting states in $k$-space and using that counting to derive thermodynamic quantities for an ideal gas. The important content is not just formula plugging: it is understanding the volume per state, degeneracy counting, the conversion from sums to integrals, and the chain of reasoning from microscopic states to $U$, $P$, $V$, $N$, and $T$.
